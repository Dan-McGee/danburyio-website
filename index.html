welcome to newhaven.io

newhaven.io is an aggregator for all hacker-related stuff going on in New Haven, CT. We’re just getting started and could use your help. If you’re interested in programming, the intertubes, and whatnot, please get in touch. We started a Google Group and are on Twitter at @newhavenio.

We hope to turn this website into a dynamic aggregator of hacker-related news and events in and around New Haven, CT, but at the moment, we are manually posting to a static site. If you want to help us build something here, please start contributing to our repo on GitHub.

newhaven.io is made by people from:

  • NewHaven.rb
  • Whiteboard Software Developer Community
