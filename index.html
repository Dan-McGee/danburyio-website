welcome to newhaven.io

newhaven.io is an aggregator for all hacker-related stuff going on in New Haven, CT. We’re just getting started and could use your help. If you’re interested in programming, the intertubes, and whatnot, please get in touch. Please follow us on Twitter at @newhavenio; subscribe to our Google Group; and join the Meetup.com Group.

If you want to help us build something here, please start contributing to our repo on GitHub.

newhaven.io is made by people from:

  • Digital Surgeons
  • NewHaven.rb
  • A100 Developer Community
